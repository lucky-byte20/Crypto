üîê What is CFB?

CFB (Cipher Feedback) is a mode of AES that turns a block cipher into a stream cipher by feeding back part of the previous ciphertext block into the encryption process.

üîÅ How CFB works?

The IV is encrypted with the key, then XORed with the plaintext block to get ciphertext; this ciphertext becomes feedback for the next block.
